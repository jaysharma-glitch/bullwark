ABOUT

We are a boutique company & have a combined experience of over 10 plus years in Hospitality, Production & Events Management between both the partners.


Coming from the hospitality industry we realized the need for Sanitization Services in the coming year & how we can, with our experiences, make the process of prevention against the pandemic more organized and Efficient.


Here, at The-Bullwark the health and safety of all our staff and clients comes first. During, this frightening time we would like to reassure all clients that we are taking the correct precautions to ensure your safety when returning to the workplace. We have tied up with all certified manufacturers for disinfectants & equipment.


SERVICES

Location/Set Sanitization

Disinfecting & sanitising using state of the art Sanitizing Equipment is what we do!

Services

  • Corner to corner disinfection of the Studio
  • Disinfect the Set, props, furniture etc.
  • Disinfect all kinds of equipment, vanity vans, toilets, makeup rooms etc.
  • External surface sanitizer wall, doors etc
  • Our products are 100% safe for humans

Entry/Exit Management and Reporting

A single entry/Exist point is the key to any sanitization process.

Services

  • Location lockdown with the help of queue managers & caution Tapes
  • Thermal temperature check
  • Queuing system & Crowd management maintaining social distancing norms
  • Detailed attendance log maintained daily
  • Disinfection Tunnel
  • Isolation and guidance of person with symptoms

Sanitization of all equipment, packages, Material, etc

In a shoot a lot of Equipment that goes in and out of your set.

Services

  • Sanitization by Spraying (ULV)
  • Sanitization by Fogging
  • Sanitization by UV for electronics and smaller packages
  • Sanitization by Swabs for expensive and precious material

Monitoring & Refilling

Our team members will ensure they constantly remind everyone of social distancing norms.

Services

  • Maintain appropriate Distance
  • Always wear your PPE kits
  • Sanitize your hands regularly
  • Do not touch surfaces if not required
  • Maintain hygiene & cleanliness
  • Refill the sanitization stands
  • Disposal of used kits
  • Medical kits

PPE kit Distribution and Management

We ensure the most efficient use of your PPE kits

Services

  • Suggesting the best kit as per the nature of the job
  • Maintaining and documenting the issue of each with with personal details (name, number, Dept, Etc.)
  • Collection & Disposal of each kit at the end of the day

Product Supply

Products
